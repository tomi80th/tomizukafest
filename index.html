Our Guest of Honor

Honoring a Lifetime of Achievement

Join us in celebrating the 80th birthday of Professor Masayoshi Tomizuka. This special occasion gathers alumni, colleagues, students, and friends from around the world to honor his monumental contributions to the fields of mechanical systems, control theory, and robotics.

Affectionately known as "Tomi" by many of his students, Professor Tomizuka has mentored generations of engineers and researchers throughout his distinguished career. This event will serve as a wonderful opportunity to reconnect, share memories, and celebrate his enduring legacy.

Please save the date! More detailed information regarding the program and registration will be announced soon.
