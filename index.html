Join us in celebrating Professor Masayoshi Tomizuka's half-century achievements. This special occasion gathers alumni, colleagues, students, and friends from around the world to honor his contributions to the control of mechanical systems.

Professor Tomizuka has mentored generations of researchers and engineers throughout his distinguished career. This event will serve as a wonderful opportunity to reconnect, share memories, and celebrate his enduring legacy.

Presentations from distinguished alumni and colleagues celebrating Prof. Tomizuka's research impact.
